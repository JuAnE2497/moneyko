Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Money Ko Dashboard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application interacts with your data. Our application operates strictly within your Google Account ecosystem.

2. How We Handle Your Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit your financial data to any external servers.

Money Ko Dashboard is a client-side interface that acts as a bridge to your personal Google Drive. When you input transactions or budgets, the data is saved directly to a Google Sheet located inside your own Google Drive. The developers of Money Ko Dashboard have zero access to this spreadsheet.

3. Google API Services Disclosure

To function, Money Ko Dashboard requests access to your Google Drive to create and read the specific spreadsheet used by the application.

Money Ko Dashboard's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your passwords. Google handles all authentication.
  • We do not see your email address or account details.
  • We do not track your financial transactions.
